Privacy Policy
Last Updated: [April 27, 2025]

This Chrome/Edge extension respects your privacy. Here's how your data is handled:

What We Collect
- When you click the extension button, it captures a screenshot of the current webpage you are viewing.
- The screenshot is then sent to a CompreFace instance that you have selected and configured.

What We Don't Collect
- We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
- We do not track your browsing activity or usage habits.
- We do not transmit any data to external servers other than the CompreFace instance you specify.

Storage
- The extension stores the CompreFace instance URL and API key **locally** in your Edge browser's extension storage.
- This information is not shared or accessible by anyone but you.

Your Control
- You are in full control of which CompreFace server is used.
- You can clear or update your settings at any time via the extension's settings page.
- You can stop using the extension at any time, and uninstall it from your browser.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out.
